# Social Media for Devs: How to Build Your Personal Brand on Autopilot

As a developer, your personal brand is your resume. But who has time to tweet and post on LinkedIn all day? Automation is the solution.

## The Developer Brand Stack

### 1. Cross-Posting
Write once, post everywhere. Use tools to automatically sync your Twitter posts to LinkedIn and Mastodon.

### 2. Evergreen Content Recycling
Don't let your best posts die. Use automation to reshuffle and repost your top-performing content every few months.

### 3. Automated Notifications
Set up alerts for when people mention your favorite tech stack or project so you can jump into the conversation at the right time.

## Why it Matters
Consistency is the most important part of social media. Automation ensures you stay active even when you're deep in a coding session.

## Start Today
Pick one platform and automate one post a day. You'll be surprised how quickly your audience grows.
