# Mastering n8n: The Low-Code Powerhouse for Automation

While tools like Zapier and Make are popular, developers often crave more control, privacy, and cost-effectiveness. Enter n8n—the extendable workflow automation tool that lets you connect anything to everything.

## Why n8n is Different

### Self-Hosted vs Cloud
n8n can be self-hosted, meaning your data never leaves your servers. This is crucial for businesses with strict privacy requirements.

### Fair-Code License
n8n is open-source at its core, allowing you to see how it works and even contribute to its ecosystem of nodes.

### Node-Based Logic
The visual interface makes it easy to map out complex logic, but you can always drop into JavaScript for advanced data transformation.

## Example Use Cases

- **Automated Customer Support**: Connect your email to an AI classifier and then route to Slack or a CRM.
- **Content Distribution**: Automatically post your latest blog to Twitter, LinkedIn, and Mastodon.
- **Data Syncing**: Keep your marketing database and production database in perfect sync.

## Conclusion
n8n is the "Swiss Army Knife" of automation. If you can imagine it, you can probably build it with n8n.
